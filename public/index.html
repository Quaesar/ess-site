About Us

The Engineering Students’ Society (ESS) works hard everyday to ensure you have the information and services you need. This website contains a collection of resources to help you - academic, social and everything in between.

Our Mission: The ESS advocates for engineering students and promotes a strong sense of community Mandate and Goals: For every engineering student to be supported socially, academically, and professionally. ESS will represent student voices while maintaining accountability and transparency..
